Fleet


SkyBros has some of the finest, modern training aircraft available in the world today and operates a fleet of up to 30 aircraft. Additionally, our aircraft are maintained to the manufacturers standards by our on-site, factory-trained technicians to ensure the highest possible safety standards are met at all times. Our fleet includes Beechcraft Duchess, Cessna SPs, Cessna 172 P/Ns, Cessna 152s, Piper Seneca III Turbo. A variety of aircraft provides students with a well rounded experience and makes them safer, better pilots.

Facilities


Our offices and teaching facilities are designed to help you progress as quickly as possible. The Redbird is a high-quality, feature-rich motion flight simulator which provides technologically advanced flight training in a safe and secure environment. It offers a unique training experience for students. It includes wrap-around visuals, a fully enclosed cockpit, reconfigurable construction, and a motion platform.

About Us

Whether you're looking for a change of career or are just starting out, SkyBros International Flight Academy can help you become a qualified, EASA approved commercial pilot in record time. Choosing SkyBros means completing the flight hours and training required to qualify as a commercial pilot faster than at other flight schools. Because we are EASA part 141 certified, instead of 200 hours of flight time being required for our students you can qualify in just 111 hours when they fly as pilot monitoring also. Plus, we offer competitive prices and can even help with organising financing so you can get started with your training immediately upon successful application. Internationally recognised and with course options to suite your goals and flexible, affordable fees, contact us now to make your dream of becoming a pilot a reality.
